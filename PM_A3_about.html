About Me

I’m Pratyush — born in Nepal, now building digital things while studying IT at Charles Sturt University. I love turning simple ideas into beautiful websites that just work.

  • Big fan of late-night design sessions and cold coffee
  • Biased toward Squarespace — can’t help it, it’s too clean
  • Once built a site just because I liked the font
  • Love quiet playlists, mountain views, and clean UI
  • One day, I’ll design a site on a rooftop café in Kathmandu

If you need a website that’s minimal, functional, and a little bit personal — let’s make it happen.

I also have a sharp eye when it comes to visual presentation. Whether it’s selecting the perfect photo, adjusting colors for the right mood, or aligning elements for balance, I pay attention to the small details that make a big difference. I believe that the way a website looks should complement how it feels — and I always aim to make things not just work well, but look right too.
